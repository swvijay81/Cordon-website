Cordon CT-100

Vehicle Trackers


CT-100 is an advanced tracker with internal GNSS & GSM antennas & battery. CT-100 efficiently covers industries like Logistics, Delivery transport, and more. It can work with a 1-wire interface which is extensively used in Refrigerated Transport applications.

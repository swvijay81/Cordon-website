AIS-140 (Vehicle Tracking Device)


An ICAT-certified AIS140 vehicle tracker with inbuilt IRNSS and GPS having RS232 interface for external data reading, Bluetooth 5.0 Connectivity with Multiple I/Os eSIM with dual network profile and CAN Interface.

Internal Battery Backup

IP65
